    What is this?

    {% call editable.region('topics-intro') %}

    The LA Counts community is focused on improving the lives of all Angelenos through open data and stories. The six topics given prominence here—Education, Environment, Health, Housing, Immigration, and Transportation—represent areas of concern that traditionally have been overlooked, underreported, or underfunded. Each topic category is an opportunity for you to reflect on the amount of available data, interrogate existing approaches to solutions, work with others to explore new ideas and applications, or write data stories that reflect the current social or political status of an issue.
